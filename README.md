# Business Requirements Document
## Automated Attendance Management System — College Portal

### Project Overview
A professional-grade Business Requirements Document (BRD) developed
as part of a Business Analyst portfolio project. This BRD proposes a
digital solution to replace the manual attendance management process
at MIT World Peace University.


###  Problem Statement (AS-IS)
The current attendance process at MIT-WPU has critical gaps:
- Attendance marked on paper with 48-hour portal entry delays
- Students have no real-time visibility into their attendance
- No automated alerts when attendance drops below 75%
- Manual report compilation before every exam — error prone

###  Proposed Solution (TO-BE)
- Faculty marks attendance digitally in real time
- Students get live subject-wise attendance dashboard
- Automated SMS and email alerts at 80% and 75% thresholds
- Online correction and approval workflow with audit trail
- One-click instant eligibility report for exam department
